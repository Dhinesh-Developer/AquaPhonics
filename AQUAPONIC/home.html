SMART AQUAPONICS SYSTEM

This project is a smart aquaponics system prototype that automates water flow between three tanks: a fish tank, a filter tank, and a plant tank. Using the ESP8266 NodeMCU, the system controls three pumps that manage the circulation of water between the tanks. The user can access a web interface to remotely control the water pumps and adjust the water flow through the tanks for optimal fish and plant growth.

The Wi-Fi connectivity allows the user to interact with the system via a smartphone, making it convenient to monitor and manage the aquaponics ecosystem from anywhere within the network. The system supports three main stages of water circulation:

  • Fish tank to filter tank
  • Filter tank to plant tank
  • Plant tank back to the fish tank

The project leverages IoT technology, enhancing the sustainability and efficiency of home-based or small-scale aquaponics farming systems. It focuses on resource-saving practices by ensuring continuous water circulation, reducing manual intervention, and optimizing the growth of fish and plants together in a symbiotic environment.
